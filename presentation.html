2-12-2018

Finding 1

Student body size drecreased until most recent school year, while referrals and suspensions increased until most recent year, when each pattern reversed.

Finding 2

Substantial increase in the percent of White students.

Finding 3

Test scores decrease when the student body is more African-American, but increase with each other category.

Areas for further exploration

Changes in neighborhood context

  • Steady increase in White students and decrease in African-American students suggest changing neighorhood demographics

Changes in district policy

  • Steady decrease then increase of student body size in most recent year suggests change in district policy such as catchment zone or nearby school closing

Changes in school context

  • Changes in discipline referrals suggest change in leadership or discipline policy

Strategic plan

Explore additional data

  • Examine disaggregated test score and discipline information
  • Explore opportunities for additional data collection especially teacher observations and progress monitoring

Turnaround Principle 6: Safe school environment

Rationale

  • High rates of discipline referrals and suspension
  • Decrease in most recent year may or may not reflection positive change

Work with principal to:

  • Understand discipline procedures and systems
  • Explore disagreggated discipline data and trends over time

Turnaround Principle 6: Safe school environment

  • Conduct surveys of staff, students, and families
  • Implement school-wide positive behavioral supports
  • Encourage restorative practices

Parent tip

Academic Parent-Teacher Teams (APTT)

  • Focus on specific standards
  • Review data about student achievement
  • Parents set SMART goals for students
  • Teachers share strategies for practicing skils at home
  • Inviting parents to participate in school
